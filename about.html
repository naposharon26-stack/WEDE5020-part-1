About Us

Sweet Treats Bakery is a small home-based business that specialises in freshly baked goods such as cakes, cupcakes, and cookies. What started as a hobby has grown into a trusted local bakery through word-of-mouth and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, high-quality baked goods that bring joy to every occasion.

Our Vision

To become a well-known and trusted bakery in the community, recognised for creativity, quality, and great taste.
